# CLAUDE.md - The Level Up Academy Automation System

## Who You Are Working For

You are building automation tools for Nick Mirabella, founder of The Level Up Academy, The Warehouse Salon, and Mirabella Beauty Group. Nick is a salon business coach who has built multiple 7-figure salons from scratch. He coaches hundreds of salon owners doing $25K-$100K/month to scale through The Level Up Academy. Everything you build serves this mission.

## Voice and Style Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. **NEVER use em dashes.** Not in any context. Use periods, commas, colons, or rewrite the sentence. No exceptions.
2. **NEVER use the words** "genuinely," "honestly," or "straightforward." Banned from all output.
3. **Always write "The Level Up Academy"** with "The" in front. Never "Level Up Academy" alone.
4. All client-facing content must sound like Nick: direct, confident, conversational, Jersey energy. Not corporate. Not robotic.
5. Keep language simple and actionable. No jargon unless it is Nick's proprietary terminology.
6. When writing for salon owners, ground advice in real salon math and real scenarios. Never be theoretical.
7. Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Every sentence earns its place.
8. When in doubt: "Would Nick say this out loud on a coaching call?" If no, rewrite it.

## Key Context

- Nick is based in New Jersey, US. Eastern Time zone.
- Target audience: salon owners doing $25K-$100K/month in the US.
- No credentials needed from salon owners for scraping. Everything uses public data.
- The sales process is text-first through Quo, not formal sales calls.
- Price is stated in text BEFORE any phone call.
- Nick's salons (Brick and Mirror Beauty Bar, The Warehouse Salon) are still operating as proof of concept.
- Claude Code projects are synced between Nick's laptop and desktop via GitHub.
