# The Business Model

## The Level Up Academy ($2,000 + $750/month)

Core coaching offer for salon owners doing $25K+/month who want to scale to $100K/month.

**Pricing:**
- $2,000 onboarding fee covers first month plus all onboarding deliverables
- $750/month ongoing starting month 2
- Month to month after that, no long-term contract
- Pay-in-full option: $8,500 for the year (saves $2,500, includes custom Shopify website build as bonus)
- 60-day Love It or Leave It guarantee: if it is not working, tear up the agreement and walk away as friends
- Results guarantee: if they do the work and do not see results, Nick works with them for free until they do

**What members get at onboarding (before first coaching call):**
- Profit First Blueprint (custom financial report from AI Accountant data)
- Visibility Blueprint (custom marketing report from Apify + Claude)
- On-site SEO fix (Claude Code analyzes and optimizes their website meta titles, descriptions, headers, schema)

**What members get ongoing:**
- 1:1 Strategy Call with Nick (60+ minutes, one time during onboarding)
- Four live weekly coaching calls (Monday Strategy, Monday Fundamentals, Tuesday Hot Seat, Wednesday Implementation)
- The Five Forces Framework curriculum in rotating 5-week cycles (10 cycles per year)
- Monthly Profit Report (billing-synced, arrives 7 days before their charge)
- Monthly Visibility Report (billing-synced, arrives 7 days before their charge)
- Monthly Client Experience / NPS Report (billing-synced, arrives 7 days before their charge)
- 24/7 private community access in Mighty Networks
- Full curriculum and advanced playbooks
- Team Training Manuals for every salon position
- Team access to Next Level Stylist (stylists, managers, front desk get their own training)
- Conversion-ready ad templates
- Shopify and e-commerce coaching (self-paced course in Mighty Networks + monthly deep-dive)
- Achievement Action Plan (updated quarterly)
- Notion Customer Portal (private dashboard with all reports, blueprints, and progress)

## Inner Circle ($3,000/month)

Premium tier for Level Up members who want done-with-you execution. Only offered to active members who are moving fast. Never sold cold.

**What they get (everything in The Level Up Academy plus):**
- Nick's team manages their Google Business Profile
- Nick's team writes blog content from their topical map
- Nick's team builds their monthly content calendar
- Nick's team uses Amplify to scrape and strategize social media, delivers custom post ideas monthly
- Nick's team optimizes their website and service pages ongoing
- Monthly 1:1 review with their Account Manager
- Monthly group strategy call with Nick (exclusive to Inner Circle)
- Monthly Marketing Strategy document generated by Claude with complete content plan, blog topics, GBP schedule, social media strategy
- Priority access to Nick in the community
- Search Atlas project activated for their domain (keyword tracking, rank monitoring, GBP analytics)

## Standalone Offers

- **Done-for-you SEO:** $6,000 upfront or $1,500/month. For salon owners who want marketing help but not coaching. Agency offer.
- **Shopify store build:** $5,000 add-on. For Level Up members who want their store built but did not go All-In.
- **Private mentorship:** $50,000/year. Limited spots. Direct access to Nick.
